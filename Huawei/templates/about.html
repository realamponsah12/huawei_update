About This Portal

This Portal is Designed by: Andy Amponsah and its purpose is to Help automate and digitalize STS processes

We Are Mommacoe

The sole purpose of this portal is to help STS leaders send realtime information and data to the STS co-ordinator from field experience from all over the country. it has a secondary purpose to keep in its DB records of information passed through it and also the details of Student Teachers who go out to observer. With this, Co-ordiantors and Link tutors will have little difficulty accessing informations such as STS Reports and other important data. This portal can further be expanded to help grade student Teachers online with further investment. with the help of this portal, 3rd party individuals can access the remarks and leadership of all Student Teachers who took the position of STS leaders and verify that what they have on thier curricullum is official We believe that individuals who hold the position of leaders should have it on their Cv's which can be easily verified by interested parties.
